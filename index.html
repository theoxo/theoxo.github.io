Theo X. Olausson

I am a Ph.D. student in computer science at MIT working with Prof. Armando Solar-Lezama. I am extremely grateful to have been supported by a Presidential Fellowship ('21). I am also co-President of the MIT EECS Graduate Students' Association.

My current research interests lie in neurosymbolic AI, (neural) program synthesis, and more generally the intersection of machine learning, programming and cognitive science.

Before starting my PhD, I graduated at the top of my class with a Master's degree in Informatics from the University of Edinburgh.
