Theo Olausson

I study and research informatics at the University of Edinburgh.
I currently hold a funded studentship from the Institute for Computing Systems Architecture.

My interests broadly revolve around applying mathematical techniques to difficult problems in computer science.

Long-term, I hope to develop techniques which combine the strong guarantees of formal methods with the practical usefulness of machine learning so that we can create artificially intelligent systems which are safe, fair, and efficient.
